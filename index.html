Ganpati Event

Event Details

Date: 19 September 2023

Sthapna Time: 11Am onwards

Events Time: 8Pm -11Pm

Location:Ganesh Nagar Colony

Games

Various games are arranged as followed.

Day 1: Ganpati Sthapna, also known as Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesha is welcomed adorned with flowers, garlands, and offerings. The atmosphere is filled with devotion and fervor as people perform rituals, chant hymns, and conduct aarti to seek blessings from Lord Ganesha.

Day 2: Drawing and Painting Competitions

Children and adults create artworks related to Lord Ganesha and the festival.

Day 3: Ganpati Idol Making Competition

Participants compete to create the most artistic and innovative clay idols of Lord Ganesha.

Day 4: Cultural Performances

Participants showcase their talents in singing, dancing, or dramatic performances related to Ganpati and Indian culture.

Day 5:Rangoli Competitions

Participants create intricate and colorful rangoli designs using colored powders and other materials.

Day 6:Cooking Competitions

People participate in cooking contests to prepare traditional delicacies and sweets associated with the festival.

Day 7:Musical chair

Musical chairs is a game where players walk around chairs in a circle to music. When the music stops, they must quickly find a chair, and one chair is removed each round until only one player remains.

Day 8:Antakshari

A musical game where participants take turns singing songs based on a specific theme or starting letter.

Day 9:Prize distribution to winners of the games.

Prize distribution is a rewarding ceremony where individuals or teams are honored for their remarkable achievements, encouraging further success and inspiring others.

Day 10:Ganpati Visarjan.

Ganesh Visarjan marks the immersion of Lord Ganesha's idol in water, concluding the joyous ten-day festival with heartfelt farewells.
